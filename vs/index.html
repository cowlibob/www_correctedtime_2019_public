PYS Handicap Comparisons

Explore differences in corrected times between dinghies with PYS ratings. Portsmouth Yardstick Scheme is an empirical handicap system used in the UK and elsewhere.

Each year, the values are adjusted based on race results from the previous year.

CorrectedTime - VS allows you to find when two PYS classes must finish in relation to each other.

For example, A Laser 4.7 finishing at 60m 0s will need to be 9m 4s ahead of a double handed Mirror to win.

To use, choose your competing classes, scroll the wheel to set the elapsed time for the first boat.
